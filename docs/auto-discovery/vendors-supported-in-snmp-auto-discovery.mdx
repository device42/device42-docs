Device42 aims to support as many vendors as possible for SNMP-based discovery. The table below lists the vendors that have been verified.

This list is not all-inclusive. Even if a vendor is not listed, discovery will generally still bring in some information, even if your specific hardware has not yet been added.

## Add Support for Non-Verified Vendors

If you come across a device that is not fully discovered or if you have hardware that you want to see supported, you can [generate an SNMP walk of the device](/administration/appliance-manager/collecting-snmpwalk-output-for-troubleshooting.mdx) by navigating to **Application > Generate SNMP Template** in your Appliance Manager.

Fill out the form, attach your MIB files, and select the standard data you want added. The completed form is routed to the Device42 engineering team for inclusion.
