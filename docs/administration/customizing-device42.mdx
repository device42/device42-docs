Device42 lets you personalize the login screen with your company logo and a custom message for users. This page covers how to upload a logo and add user instructions via Global Settings.

## Customize the Login Screen

Navigate to **Tools > Settings > Global Settings** and scroll down to the **Login screen** section.

Upload user instructions to display a custom message on the login screen, such as access policies or contact information for support.
