In this HowTo, we cover Backing up and Restoring your Device42 database. You do this from https://DEVICE42URL:4242, default credentials \[ d42admin / default \]. Follow along & learn how to both manually backup & restore, and how to schedule backups to NFS, SFTP, and even an Amazon S3 bucket.
