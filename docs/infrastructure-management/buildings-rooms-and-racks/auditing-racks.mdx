---
title: "Auditing Racks"
sidebar_position: 1
---

You can export rack data from Device42 to an Excel spreadsheet and use a barcode reader to audit physical devices against the records in Device42.

Navigate to the Racks list view in Device42:

![Browse to Racks](/assets/images/2016-02-26-rack-audit-01.png)

Select the racks you want to audit, then choose **Audit Export for Selected Racks** from the **Actions** dropdown menu:

![Audit Export Racks](/assets/images/2016-02-26-rack-audit-03.png)

Save the spreadsheet when prompted and open it in Excel.

![Audit Spreadsheet](/assets/images/2016-02-26-rack-audit-04.png)

With the spreadsheet open, use a barcode reader to scan devices in the rack by asset tag and compare them to the information in Device42, updating the spreadsheet to reflect any changes as you move through the racks.
