# Infrastructure

Device42 lets you manage and track all of your infrastructure and provides a full DCIM solution.

You can use Device42 to manage all the physical or virtual components that make up your infrastructure. This includes but is not limited to:

* Hardware and Devices
* Software and Operating Systems
* IP addresses
* Purchasing agreements and contracts

This section of the documentation details how to add and manage your infrastructure using Device42. If you're looking for a higher-level overview of Device42's infrastructure management features, take a look at our [Data Center Management feature page](https://www.device42.com/features/data-center-management/). To dive deeper into what we offer, take a look at this category's subpages in the sidebar.
