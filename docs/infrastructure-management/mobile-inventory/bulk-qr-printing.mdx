You can bulk print QR codes or barcodes from the devices or assets list views.

## Create QR/Barcode Profile

First, create a QR or Barcode profile. 

Navigate to **Tools > Templates & Bulk Operations > QR Profiles** and choose between QR and Barcode for the **Code Type**.

## Bulk Print QR Codes or Barcodes

Navigate to the **All Devices** or **All Assets** list page under the **Resources** menu.  

- Search or filter for the devices or assets you would like to print QR codes or barcodes for and select the items.
- From the **Actions** Menu, choose **Generate QR/Barcodes for selected items**.

    <ThemedImage
    alt="Generate QR codes action"
    sources={{
        light: useBaseUrl('/assets/images/bulk-qr-printing/generate-qr-codes-action-light.png'),
        dark: useBaseUrl('/assets/images/bulk-qr-printing/generate-qr-codes-action-dark.png'),
    }}
    />

- In the **Choose QR profile for selected items** field, select the QR code (or barcode) profile you prefer.

    <ThemedImage
    alt="Choose QR profile"
    sources={{
        light: useBaseUrl('/assets/images/bulk-qr-printing/choose-qr-profile-light.png'),
        dark: useBaseUrl('/assets/images/bulk-qr-printing/choose-qr-profile-dark.png'),
    }}
    />

- The next page will preview the codes according to the profile settings you chose followed by the **Print** button.

    ![QR codes print preview](/assets/images/bulk-qr-printing/qr-codes-print-preview-2.png)

### Tips

- Select the **Cut at the end** option in the printer preferences.
- Bulk print the QR codes or barcodes in batches of up to 100 at a time for better performance.
