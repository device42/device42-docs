---
title: "Devices"
---

Devices are the core objects in Device42, representing physical servers, virtual machines, blade chassis, clusters, and other compute resources. Most device records are populated automatically through discovery jobs, but you can also add and edit devices manually.

This section covers how to view, add, edit, and clone devices, along with details on device types, hardware models, lifecycle tracking, and other device properties. Use the sidebar to navigate to specific topics.
