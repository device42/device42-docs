The SNMP discovery process automatically finds any environmental sensors attached to your PDUs, including temperature and humidity sensors.

## Discover Environmental Sensors via PDUs

Power units are discovered via the SNMP discovery option in the **Discovery** menu. See the **Power Unit Autodiscovery** section of [Getting Started with Power and Environmental Monitoring](/infrastructure-management/power-and-environmental-monitoring/getting-started-with-power-and-environmental-monitoring.mdx) for more information.

## View Discovered Sensors

When sensors are discovered, they are created with an asset record in Device42. To view all discovered sensors, navigate to **Resources > All Assets** and filter the **Type** by **Sensor**.
