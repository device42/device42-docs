These recipes give infrastructure, operations, and finance teams a detailed view of physical and virtual compute assets. Use them for capacity planning, hardware refresh planning, chargeback reporting, and identifying data quality gaps, such as [devices missing a type or service level classification](/reports/dbb-cookbook/compute/devices-missing-device_type-or-service_level).
