These recipes are for security, compliance, and audit teams who need to surface risk across the environment. They identify devices with [external exposure](reports/dbb-cookbook/security/devices-accessed-by-external-ips.mdx), [prohibited software](reports/dbb-cookbook/security/devices-with-prohibited-software-as-designated-by-customer.mdx), [commonly exploited ports](reports/dbb-cookbook/security/devices-using-commonly-exploited-ports.mdx), [PII risks](reports/dbb-cookbook/security/devices-possibly-containing-pii-that-have-public-ips.mdx), and [prod or non-prod isolation violations](reports/dbb-cookbook/security/devices-with-mismatch-between-prod-and-non-prod-environments.mdx), as well as discovery gaps where missing data creates blind spots.
