These recipes are for teams planning infrastructure changes, migrations, or maintenance who need to understand the downstream business impact. They map how applications depend on devices and how devices support applications, helping you answer questions like "what breaks if this server goes down?" ([Business App Device Impacts](/reports/dbb-cookbook/app-dependency-mapping/business-app-device-impacts)) or "which apps are at risk from this hardware EOL?" ([Business App Hardware EOL/EOS Impact](/reports/dbb-cookbook/app-dependency-mapping/business-app-hardware-eol-eos-impact)).
